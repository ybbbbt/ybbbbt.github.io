Bangbang Yang

杨镑镑

I am currently a researcher and developer at PICO Mixed Reality group in the ByteDance Ltd. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the ZJU3DV group of State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University in 2022, advised by Prof. Guofeng Zhang and Prof. Zhaopeng Cui. Before that, I got my bachelor degree from Hefei University of Technology.

I have a broader interest in 3D vision/graphics and its AR/VR applications, including 3D content generation and editing (3D AIGC), neural rendering, 3D scene understanding.
